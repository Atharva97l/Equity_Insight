# Equity_Insight
Building a stock intelligence platform for fundamental analysis, valuation, risk assessment, and investor research.

Goal:
the platform aims to answer - is company/stock finanacilly healthy ,overvalued , undervalued , risk assesment ,analyze companies through this factors and financial statements,valuation models , automated research reports
Show results with interactive dashboards Display- Revenue Trends, Profit trends,..

Ai research reports -
generate Investment thesis , financial health summary ,Risk summary ,.. using LLM API's

Roadmap::-
Phase 1- Data collection
Fetch company financial data

Tasks: learn financial Api's,Connect Api's,retrive company fundamentals,store data in pandas

Phase 2 - Analytics Engine :
Perform financial analysis 

ex - financial ratios , growth calculations, company scoring

Phase 3 - Valuation Engine :
Estimate intrinsic Value 

ex- dcf model , relative valuation , margin of safety 

Phase 4 - Dahsboard 

visualize analysis 

tasks - charts , company pages ,interactive dashboard

generate investment research reports
